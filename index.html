About Me

Working in a labor focused profession for the past decade most of which as a co-operator of a small business has given me the ability to meet deadlines, work with many groups to accomplish one common goal, and understand what hard work means. Pushing me to pursue a career in which I have always had a secret crush on as a developer, I have done well with front end design matching code to design styles. Attending a coding bootcamp has given me more exposure styling using CSS, and experience using javaScript. I enjoy troubleshooting, and figuring out when, why, and how things are working while learning and intaking as much information as possible. I would consider myself a hard worker who is willing to put in max effort and time on projects, whether by myself or in a group. For this reason I am looking forward to paying my dues while learning and honing my craft going forward.
